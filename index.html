The raid, conducted early this morning, resulted in the arrest of ten individuals suspected of orchestrating a series of sophisticated cyber attacks. These attacks targeted both local businesses and international organizations, resulting in substantial financial losses.

The suspects, believed to be part of an organized network, were found in possession of numerous high-tech devices, including laptops, smartphones, and hard drives containing sensitive data.
